RKO Live TV

Android App with Floating Support

🎬 Picture-in-Picture - Video floats while using other apps
📺 Full Screen - Immersive video experience
⚡ Fast Load - Built-in WebView, no browser needed
🔒 Secure - No data collection, your privacy protected
Version: 1.0.0 | Size: ~5MB | Requires: Android 7+

⚠️ Build Instructions

  1. Download the android/ folder from GitHub
  2. Open in Android Studio (free from google.com/android-studio)
  3. Click Build → Build APK
  4. Transfer APK to phone and install

Note: APK cannot be hosted on website due to Google Play policies. Build locally or use Android Studio.
